Braskadi

Russian Imperial Stout

/10,5% ABV

A cross cultural collaboration with the Brazilian brewery Perro Libre where we remixed the Imperial Stout. Cacao nibs, Palo Santo wood, and raisins invite intrigue, imagination and respect while shunning indifference.

Ingredients: Water, malts, oat flakes, cocoa nibs, palo santo wood, raisins, hops and yeast.

Pairings: Chocolate desserts, ice-cream, tiramisu.

Glass: Snifter.

What's the meaning of "Rebel level"?

"Rebel level" is the laugarian scale to measure the quantity of naughty, irreverence or power in their beers. An indicator defined by the laugarian crew itself, which will give you a clue as to what you are going to enjoy.
