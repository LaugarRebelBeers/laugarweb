Visit the Taproom

Make yourself at home and join us to sample our beers, eat some food or even take some beers home. Core beer range beers and the most experimental ones are combined in our taps in the best atmosphere.

We open on fridays and saturdays from 6pm to 11:30pm.

The taproom is located at the brewery itself and it is 2 minutes walk from the Bizkaibus A3342 bus stop. (Routes and timetables)
